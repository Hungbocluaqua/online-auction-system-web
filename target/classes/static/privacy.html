Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect username, email address, and account activity data. We also collect auction listings, bid history, and payment records.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to operate the auction platform, process transactions, send notifications, and improve our service.

3. Data Storage

Data is stored in an embedded H2 database. Session tokens are stored with expiry (24 hours absolute, 30 minutes idle timeout).

4. Data Retention

Finished auctions are retained indefinitely for record-keeping. You may request account deletion at any time, which removes all associated data.

5. Security

Passwords are hashed using BCrypt (cost 12). Sessions use HttpOnly cookies with CSRF protection. Account lockout occurs after 5 failed login attempts.

6. Your Rights

You may view, update, or delete your account data at any time. You may also request a copy of all data associated with your account.

7. Contact

For privacy inquiries, please contact the system administrator through the platform.
